- Llista el total de factures d'un client/a en un període determinat.
- List the total number of invoices for a customer within a specific period.

USE OPTIC;
SELECT * 
FROM sale s
JOIN clients c ON s.clients_client_id = c.client_id
WHERE c.name = 'Marta Ruiz' 
	AND s.registration_sale BETWEEN '2024-12-01' AND '2025-08-01' 

- Llista els diferents models d'ulleres que ha venut un empleat/da durant un any.
- List the different models of glasses that an employee has sold during a given year.

USE OPTIC;
SELECT g.brand, 
g.color_lens, 
g.price, 
g.glasses_frame,
s.registration_sale
FROM sale s
JOIN glasses g ON s.glasses_glasses_id = g.glasses_id
WHERE s.name_employee = 'Javier López' 
	AND s.registration_sale BETWEEN '2024-10-10'AND '2025-10-10'  


- Llista els diferents proveïdors que han subministrat ulleres venudes amb èxit per l'òptica.
- List the different suppliers who have provided glasses that were successfully sold by the optical store.

USE OPTIC;
SELECT DISTINCT s.name AS Supplier,
g.brand AS Brand,
a.name_client AS Client
FROM supplier s
JOIN glasses g ON s.supplier_id = g.supplier_supplier_id
JOIN sale a ON a.glasses_glasses_id =  g.glasses_id
