1.- Llista quants productes de categoria 'Begudes' s'han venut en una determinada localitat.
	List how many products in the 'Drinks' category have been sold in a specific location.

SELECT 
    ci.name AS ciudad,
    p.type as Tipo,
    SUM(op.quantity) AS total_Bebidas_venta
FROM orders_product op
JOIN product p ON op.product_id = p.product_id
JOIN orders o ON op.orders_id = o.orders_id
JOIN client c ON o.client_id = c.client_id
JOIN city ci ON c.city_id = ci.city_id
WHERE p.type = 'drink'
  AND ci.name = 'Barcelona'  
GROUP BY ci.name;


2. - Llista quantes comandes ha efectuat un determinat empleat/da.
	List how many orders a specific employee has processed.

SELECT e.name, COUNT(o.orders_id) AS total_orders
FROM orders o
JOIN employee e ON o.employee_id = e.employee_id
GROUP BY e.employee_id;
